RULES OF THE GAME

  1. A word or phrase is given to you.
  2. Your objective is to guess the word or phrase before your man gets hung!
  3. You have to try to guess what it is one letter at a time.
  4. The number of dashes are provided equivalent to the number of letters in the word.
  5. You have to type a letter from your keyboard that you think might occur in the word.
  6. If the word does not contain the input letter, one element of a hangman's gallows is drawn.
  7. As the game progresses, a segment of the gallows and of a victim is added for every suggested letter not in the word.
  8. The number of incorrect guesses are 5. After which you have to log out and login again in order to play again
